Deployment Orders

HOW TO PLAY

Getting underway with Convoy 1943 requires a few preparations. Follow these steps to deploy into the North Atlantic.

01

INSTALL STORMWORKS

You'll need Stormworks: Build and Rescue on Steam. Ensure your game is updated to the latest version for shader and Lua compatibility.

02

SUBSCRIBE TO THE MOD

Find Convoy 1943 on the Steam Workshop. Subscribe to the main addon and any required vehicle packs. The shader pack is a separate download.

03

DEPLOY & ENGAGE

Load the Convoy 1943 save, select your escort vessel, and report for duty. Use radio channels for coordination and ASDIC to hunt submarines.
