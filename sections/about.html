About the Mission

THE NORTH ATLANTIC AWAITS

Convoy 1943 is a comprehensive naval warfare simulation built within Stormworks: Build and Rescue. Set during the pivotal year of the Battle of the Atlantic, it places you at the helm of escort vessels tasked with shepherding convoys through U-boat infested waters.

Every system is hand-crafted for authenticity — from the ASDIC sonar pings echoing through your hull, to the weather-beaten seas of the North Atlantic rendered through custom shaders. This is submarine warfare as it was: tense, unforgiving, and utterly immersive.
