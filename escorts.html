Player Controllable Escorts

Flower Corvette (Fully Upgraded)

A versatile and reliable escort vessel, the Flower-class corvette was a mainstay in the Battle of the Atlantic. This upgraded version is equipped for serious anti-submarine warfare.

Key Features:

  • Hydrophone
  • Active Sonar
  • Radar
  • Two Rear Depth Charge Racks
  • Two Port Depth Charge Throwers
  • Two Starboard Depth Charge Throwers
  • Base Max Speed 16kts

SC-497 Class Submarine Chaser

A quick and agile wooden-hulled submarine chaser, the SC-497 class was designed for coastal anti-submarine patrols. Its speed and maneuverability make it a nimble hunter.

Key Features:

  • Hydrophone
  • Six Single Holder Depth Charge Racks
  • One Port Depth Charge Thrower
  • One Starboard Depth Charge Thrower
  • Base Max Speed 15.5 kts or 21 kts depending on Engine Configuration.
