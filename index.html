Mission Briefing: About Convoy 1943

Convoy 1943 is a meticulously crafted Stormworks: Build and Rescue addon that thrusts you into the heart of World War II's Battle of the Atlantic. Take command of escort vessels, manage resources, and defend vital supply convoys against relentless enemy attacks.

This addon focuses on providing an immersive and challenging experience, blending historical inspiration with Stormworks' unique physics and building capabilities. Prepare for dynamic weather, intelligent enemy AI, and missions that will test your strategic thinking and piloting skills.
