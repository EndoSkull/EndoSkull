Addon Command Roster

{/* Increased max-width for more space */}

Below is a list of available commands to enhance your experience with the Convoy 1943 addon. Familiarize yourself with these orders to effectively manage your missions and assets.

Team Commands

  • ?join [axis/allies] Used to join a team. Example: ?join allies
  • ?leave Used to leave current team. However, you can switch teams at anytime by using the ?join command. Example: ?leave
  • ?tp [id] / ?tp [name] Teleport to the specified vehicle, if it’s on the same team as you. Example: ?tp 27 or ?tp EggsShip

Convoy Related Commands

  • ?convoy [wp1] [wp2] Spawn a convoy between the two specified waypoints. Example: ?convoy 1 5
  • ?spawn [vehicle] [quantity] Use the optional quantity argument to create formations of vehicles. Example: ?spawn Type-IIA or ?spawn Foxhound 2

Player Vehicle Related Commands

  • ?empty [ship] Spawn selected ship without NPC crewmembers. Example: ?empty Type-IIA
  • ?spawn [ship] Spawn selected ship with NPC crewmembers. Example: ?spawn Type-IIA
  • ?gunners Refresh gunners if they have despawned (useful for submarines, where gunners despawn when submarine submerges). Example: ?gunners
  • ?name [id] [name] Names the ship with ID to specified name, look at top left for ID of nearest ship. Example: ?name 5 Greyhound
  • ?commandeer Remove an NPC vehicle’s captain/pilot so you can take over. Example: ?commandeer
  • ?control [vehicle] [quantity] Similar to ?spawn, but teleports you to pilot/captain seat. Currently should be used to spawn and control team scout planes and bombers. Example: ?control B17 2
  • ?bail Trigger crew to bail out of your aircraft you are currently in control of. You will be transported back to your ship after aircraft is destroyed, you are killed, or reach the ground (i think?). Example: ?bail

Team Gameplay Related Commands

  • ?resupply Request a new supply ship. Supply ships spawn 3-5km from you, and will attempt to rendezvous with your ship. Look out for messages from them, and use ?request when they’re ready. Example: ?resupply
  • ?request [first letter of commodity] Requests a commodity from a waiting supply ship. Example: ?request a (requests advanced torpedo) ?request b (requests basic torpedo) ?request h (requests hedgehog round)
  • ?dismiss Dismiss a waiting supply ship. Example: ?dismiss

Utility Related Commands

  • ?scuttle Destroy the nearest vehicle to you instantly. Useful for disabled ships. Example: ?scuttle
  • ?clear [all] Used to clear convoys or convoys and all other ships/subs. Example: ?clear (despawn convoys) ?clear all (despawn convoys and other ships/subs)
