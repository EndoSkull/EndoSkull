Stormworks Community YouTube Channels

Discover Stormworks content from talented community creators! These channels offer a range of videos, from showcases and tutorials to exciting gameplay.

454ss Gaming & Builds

Channel URL: https://www.youtube.com/@454ss_gaming

Stormworks Content: This channel offers a variety of Stormworks topics, including showcases of creations and works-in-progress (WIPs), tutorial videos to inspire other builders, and general gameplay. Popular content includes tips for new players and common mistakes to avoid. They also have playlists for "Stormworks Creation Showcases," "Stormworks Behind the Scenes," and "Stormworks Tips and Engineering." Some content delves into real-life engineering disciplines as applied in Stormworks.

Frantic (@FranticMatty)

Channel URL: https://www.youtube.com/@FranticMatty

Stormworks Content: Frantic's channel features Stormworks gameplay, often centered around building specific vehicles or exploring game updates. Examples include building rafts, WW2 bombers, and content related to the sails update. Recent videos involve scenarios like oil tanker fire rescues. Community members note a long-standing presence and a focus on ship-sinking scenarios.

EndoSkull Gaming (@EndoSkull)

Channel URL: https://www.youtube.com/@EndoSkull

Stormworks Content: Features "creative Stormworks adventures" with an emphasis on naval and military themes, including "epic convoy battles." Content includes showcases of mods like new hydrophones and active sonar, and components like viewing scopes. They also offer members-only content related to Stormworks builds and events.

MrNJersey (@MrNJersey)

Channel URL: https://www.youtube.com/@MrNJersey

Stormworks Content: Historically a very influential creator known for comprehensive tutorials, including official Stormworks tutorials on topics like hull design. He also covered significant game updates. While highly regarded, community members note that many of his tutorials are now outdated due to game updates, and he is no longer actively creating Stormworks content.

Terr Rawr Saur (@terr-rawr-saur)

Channel URL: https://www.youtube.com/@terr-rawr-saur

Stormworks Content: Focuses on straightforward, "no faffing about" tutorials. Content includes testing vehicle stability and troubleshooting game physics. Community members praise the channel for high production quality and for being a "builder's channel by a builder for builders."
